Novels Supral Read

A curated list of books Supral has read with short reviews and notes. Click any title to explore more.

Star Wars: Abyss

Author: Troy Denning

Star Wars: Abyss follows Grandmaster Luke Skywalker and his son Ben Skywalker. The story takes place decades after the original saga and explores ship graveyards, the return of old enemies, and a personal journey for Luke as he seeks answers about a cousin who may have turned to darkness.

Vampire Academy — Blood Promise

Author: Richelle Mead

Blood Promise follows Rose Hathaway as she abandons the academy to hunt Dimitri, her former instructor turned strigoi. The novel blends loyalty, sacrifice and the painful consequences of love.

Vampire Academy — Last Sacrifice

Author: Richelle Mead

The conclusive volume of Rose's story: graduation, guardianship, and the final challenges that test her courage and loyalty.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Author: J.K. Rowling

The fifth Harry Potter book sees Harry face political interference at Hogwarts, the loss of a loved one, and the rise of Voldemort's power. Themes of grief, responsibility and resistance are central.

The Old Man and the Sea

Author: Ernest Hemingway

A moving tale of an aging fisherman's struggle with nature, endurance, and dignity. Hemingway's concise prose explores resilience and human pride.

Siddhartha

Author: Hermann Hesse

Siddhartha follows a man's spiritual journey from prince to seeker to enlightened soul. It's a timeless exploration of self-discovery and the search for meaning.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Author: J.K. Rowling

The second year at Hogwarts brings secrets, a hidden chamber and a mystery that tests the courage of Harry and his friends.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Author: J.K. Rowling

A darker entry as Harry faces deadly trials in the Triwizard Tournament and witnesses the return of a terrifying enemy.

Percy Jackson (series)

Author: Rick Riordan

Percy discovers he's a demigod and embarks on dangerous quests inspired by Greek mythology — full of humor, friendship and adventure.

Guinness World Records 2024

A compendium of remarkable human and natural achievements — entertaining and informative for curious readers of all ages.

Frostbite

Author: Richelle Mead

Another installment in the Vampire Academy series featuring perilous missions, heartbreak and the growing responsibilities of the characters.

Jane Eyre

Author: Charlotte Brontë

A classic coming-of-age novel about independence, morality, and love — following Jane's life from hardship to eventual resilience.

The Power of Positive Thinking

Author: Norman Vincent Peale

A self-help classic that encourages optimistic thinking and practical steps to build confidence and resilience.

Annapurna

Author: Maurice Herzog

A mountaineering classic recounting the first ascent of Annapurna in 1950 — a story of endurance, leadership and the human spirit.
